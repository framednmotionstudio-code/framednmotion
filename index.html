Our Story

A Decade of Storytelling

Founded in 2025, Framed & Motion Studio began as a one-man photography practice in a small studio apartment. What started as a passion project has grown into a full-service photography and videography services with a team of dedicated artists.

We believe that every person, every family, and every brand has a unique story worth telling. Our mission is to translate those stories into stunning visual narratives that stand the test of time.
